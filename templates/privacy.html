Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Data We Collect

Spendly collects information that you provide directly to us. This includes expense data such as transaction amounts, categories, dates, descriptions, and any notes you add. We may also collect account information like your email address and password when you register.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data you provide to operate and improve the Spendly service. This includes generating expense summaries, category breakdowns, and spending insights. Your data is used solely for providing you with expense tracking functionality and improving your experience with the application.

3. Data Storage

Your expense data is stored securely while using the application. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, as a personal finance tracking tool, we recommend maintaining your own backups of important financial data. Data is retained as long as you maintain an active account.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. We do not share your expense information with external services except as required by law or with your explicit consent. The application may use third-party services for hosting or analytics, but your expense data remains private.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your data at any time. You can export or remove all your expense information through the application settings. If you delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed from our systems.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the application.
